RAFC Document Monitoring System

About RAFC Document Monitoring System

RAFC-DMS is a sytematic approach for gathering, monitoring and tracking of Regional Agriculture and Fisheries Council (RAFC) related documents in Caraga Region. It serves as a repository of documents at the same time tracking tool of resolutions, highlights or minutes of meetings, memorandum, special orders and other documents. It also tracks the submission of documents from the different Agriculture and Fisheries Councils (AFCs) in the Caraga Region.
